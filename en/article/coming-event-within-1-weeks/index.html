More than 100 people have registered for the event!

March 09, 2024 Hikaru Maruyama

Hello everyone, I would like to thank you all for your support. Since the event was published, the number of participants has gradually increased, and on 4 March the number of participants exceeded 100! At the time of writing, the number of participants has exceeded 130.

The event venue is laid out with 150 people in mind, but the capacity of the venue can accommodate more than 150 people. We are very grateful to LINE Yahoo for kindly lending us their lovely venue.

Whether you have been using Drupal for a long time or have just started using Drupal, let's have fun together!
