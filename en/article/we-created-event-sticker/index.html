Dries portrait sticker was created!

March 13, 2024 Hikaru Maruyama

The event is just one day away! We are making various preparations for the event and among them we have created stickers as a special offer for visitors.

As the event will be gathering around Dries, we designed a portrait of Dries. I thought that as long as I was going to distribute stickers with his portrait on them, I would need permission from him, but I only got his permission the day before the event, which was today. Dries was happy to accept it, even though it was just before the event.

Image

We look forward to distributing the officially approved portrait stickers to all visitors on the day of the event.
